Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is StayConnect?
A: StayConnect is a website dedicated to helping seniors improve their digital literacy and stay safe online.
Q: How can I get started with video calls?
A: We have tutorials for popular video chat apps like Skype, Zoom, FaceTime, and WebEx. Visit our Tech Tutorials section to get started.
Q: How do I protect myself from online scams?
A: Our Safety & Security section provides tips on how to spot and avoid common scams. We also update this section regularly with information on the latest scams.
Q: What kind of resources do you offer?
A: We offer step-by-step guides, articles, and external links to trusted resources. Check out our Resources section for more information.
Q: How can I contact StayConnect for more help?
A: You can reach us through our Contact Us page. We’re here to help!
Q: How can I stay safe while shopping online?
A: Our Shopping Safety Guide provides essential tips for secure online shopping, including how to identify trustworthy websites, use secure payment methods, and avoid phishing scams.
Q: What are the best practices for creating strong passwords?
A: Visit our Password Security page to learn how to create strong, memorable passwords and use password managers to keep your accounts safe from hackers.
Q: How can I recognize and report phishing emails?
A: Our Email Security section explains how to identify phishing emails and provides step-by-step instructions on reporting them to protect your personal information.
Q: What should I do if I suspect my computer has a virus?
A: Check out our Virus Protection guide for advice on how to detect, remove, and prevent computer viruses, including recommendations for reliable antivirus software.
Q: How can I keep my social media accounts secure?
A: Our Social Media Safety tips help you protect your privacy on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram, with advice on setting strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and managing privacy settings.
